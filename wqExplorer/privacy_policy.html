wqExplorer Privacy Policy

wqExplorer was created as a "give-back" project to help with understanding water quality data. We do not collect data within the app. We do provide a feedback mechanism that will used only to answer questions or explore suggested improvements. We collect feedback you provide through our online forms or to our support email (wqexplorer@gmail.com). This includes your written feedback and your contact information if you choose to provide it. We use your feedback to understand how to improve the app and address any issues you may have encountered. We will not share or publish your contact information without your permission.
